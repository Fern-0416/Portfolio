Reflective Make-AR In-Action - Volunteer Undergraduate Research

Time Spent: January - May 2023

Worked under Dr. Dishita Turakhia to design a program in augmented reality that gave users step-by-step instructions on how to use tools and on making projects. Additionally, the program encouranged in-action and on-action reflection, which encourages users to learn and reflect more on why they use tools in certain ways and how to avoid repeating mistakes.

My responsabilities included helping with the design and assembly of a PCP board that reads sensors on the tools used with augmented reality. Additionally, I worked on implementing speech-to-text and text-to-speech functionality with the Augmented Reality glasses, such that the user can speak to the glasses, producing text that is sent to an AI, which responds via audio output.
